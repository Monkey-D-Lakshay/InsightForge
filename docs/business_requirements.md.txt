# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Project Overview

InsightForge is an Enterprise Retail Analytics Platform built for a fictional e-commerce company called InsightMart.

The platform aims to collect raw business data, transform it through an ETL pipeline, store it in a PostgreSQL data warehouse, and provide business insights through SQL analytics and Power BI dashboards.

---

## 2. Company Background

InsightMart is a multinational online retail marketplace that connects customers with independent sellers.

The company operates across multiple regions and processes thousands of customer orders every day.

Different departments currently maintain separate reports, leading to inconsistent KPIs and delayed business decisions.

---

## 3. Business Problem

Operational data is scattered across different systems.

Because of this:

- Different teams report different revenue numbers.
- Customer information is inconsistent.
- Business reports take too long to generate.
- Executives lack a single source of truth.

The company needs a centralized analytics platform.

## 5. Business Objectives

The project should help the business:

- Centralize business data.
- Build a reliable data warehouse.
- Standardize KPIs.
- Monitor sales performance.
- Analyze customer behavior.
- Improve delivery performance.
- Support strategic business decisions.

---

## 6. Key Business Questions

Sales

- What is the monthly revenue?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which regions perform best?

Customers

- How many new customers joined each month?
- What is the repeat purchase rate?
- Who are the most valuable customers?

Products

- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which products should be discontinued?

Sellers

- Which sellers perform the best?
- Which sellers have high cancellation rates?

Logistics

- Average delivery time
- Late delivery percentage
- On-time delivery rate
