# Business Insights

The following insights were derived from the PostgreSQL analytics warehouse using SQL analysis across 98,665 orders and R$28.4M in item revenue.

---

## 1. Revenue Concentration by Product Category

The top five product categories contributed **39.48% of total revenue**.

The leading categories were:

- Beauty & Health — **9.13%**
- Watches & Gifts — **8.82%**
- Bed, Bath & Table — **7.69%**
- Sports & Leisure — **7.21%**
- Computers & Accessories — **6.63%**

### Recommendation

Prioritize inventory planning, merchandising, and promotional investment in leading categories while evaluating lower-performing categories for assortment and pricing optimization.

---

## 2. Strong Geographic Revenue Concentration

The top three states generated **63.35% of total revenue**.

São Paulo alone contributed **38.34%**, followed by Rio de Janeiro at **13.47%** and Minas Gerais at **11.54%**.

### Recommendation

Prioritize inventory availability, delivery capacity, and targeted marketing in the Southeast region while developing strategies to expand into underrepresented markets.

---

## 3. Strong Revenue Growth Over Time

Monthly revenue increased substantially during the core operating period.

Revenue grew from **R$257K in January 2017** to more than **R$2M per month during multiple months in 2018**.

### Recommendation

Use historical growth patterns to support capacity planning across inventory, sellers, logistics, and customer support as transaction volume scales.

---

## 4. Credit Cards Dominate Payment Revenue

Credit cards generated **77.23% of total revenue**, while boleto contributed **16.83%**.

Together, these two payment methods represented the overwhelming majority of transaction value.

### Recommendation

Prioritize reliability and conversion optimization for credit-card payments while maintaining strong support for boleto transactions as a significant secondary payment channel.
